About UNIFORUM

UNIFORUM is an all-in-one college platform designed to manage forums, streamline event approvals, and boost student participation. It provides a centralized system for planning, tracking, and approving events through multiple authority levels, ensuring transparency and accountability at every stage. The platform improves communication between students and authorities while reducing manual effort and delays.
